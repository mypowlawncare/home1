About Us

At POW Lawn Care our mission is to give you the best lawn care services to make you proud of yourlawn. We have been in operation since 2015 doing lawn mowing, weed whacking, edging, weeding, and other heavy outside labor in various areas such as Lehi, American Fork, Eagle Mountain, SaratogaSprings, Spanish Fork and are willing to expand to different areas.

Over the last few years our team has worked hard to be the most dependable, fast, hard working lawn care company in Utah. When you meet any member of our team you will notice they have a smile on their face and are exited to do their job and that's because they are. Each member has had up to 4 years of experience doing lawn care giving you the confidence that they'll get the job done.

Why choose us? You should choose us becasue we are fast, reliable, loyal and make it our priority to make a strong relationship with you and our team based on friendship not a paycheck.

At POW Lawn Care we hope to help you with your lawn care needs with the help of an amazing team and give you the best looking yard on the street that your proud to show off to your friends, family, and neighbors.
