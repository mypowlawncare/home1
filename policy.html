Customer Policy

   To give each and every customer the best service and to give our lawn careattendants the ability to perform their best, POW Lawn Care has a policy thatcostumers must agree to prior to the rendering of any services. The following belowoutlines our customer/lawn care attendant policy.

1. Customers must have their yard freed of materials. (i.e. toys, dog waist, rocks, etc.)

2. If the lawn is not freed of materials prior to services being rendered and the lawn care attendant picks up the materials, a $10.00 fee will be charged.

3. Customers must give lawn care attendants a warning of objects in the yard that can damaged or damage the equipment prior to any services rendered. If a lawn care attendant does happen to destroy an object that was not flagged prior to servicing a customers lawn, POW Lawn Care and the lawn care attendant is not responsible for the replacement or fixes to the broken object.

4. Animals must be inside, chained, or put into a crate before a lawn care attendant will proceed to do any services.

5. Lawn Care attendants will dispose of yard waste into customers garbage cans such as cut grass, trimmings, weeds, etc.

6. It would be greatly appreciated if garbage cans could be put in an area where it is easly accessable by the lawn care attendant prior to their arrival.

7. Lawn care attendants will not be allowed to enter into a customers house for the safety of the lawn care attendant.

8. Payment must be submitted prior to any services rendered.

9. The scheduling of an appointment through POW Lawn Care must be submitted 3 or more days prior to the admired date of services.
